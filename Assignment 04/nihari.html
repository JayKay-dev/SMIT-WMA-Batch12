Beef Nihari

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef shank
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup wheat flour
  • Spices (nihari masala, ginger, garlic, etc.)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and green chilies
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add beef and cook until it changes color.
  3. Add ginger, garlic, and nihari masala. Cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add enough water to cover the meat and cook on low heat until the beef is tender.
  5. Mix wheat flour with water to make a smooth paste and add it to the pot.
  6. Cook until the gravy thickens to desired consistency.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander, green chilies, and lemon wedges. Serve hot.
