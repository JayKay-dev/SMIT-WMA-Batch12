Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices (biryani masala, cumin, coriander, etc.)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add chicken and cook until it changes color.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, yogurt, and spices. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  4. Boil the rice until half cooked and then layer it over the chicken.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves. Serve hot.
