Suji Ka Halwa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (suji)
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 cups water
  • Cardamom powder
  • Nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • Raisins

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add semolina. Roast until golden brown.
  2. Add water and cook until the mixture thickens.
  3. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Cook until the halwa reaches desired consistency.
  4. Garnish with nuts and raisins. Serve warm.
